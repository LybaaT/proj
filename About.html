Our history

We started out as a small store in Florida, aiming to be the right house for your luggage needs. Bronx Luggage, located in the Festival Marketplace, offers a wide selection of luggage from affordable sets to the best brands on the market for all of your travel needs. We also stock Jansport Backpacks, travel accessories, briefcases and much more.
